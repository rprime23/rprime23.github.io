Clay Beats

Clay Beats is a casual couch co-op rhythm game where two players take on two kindred cats who aim to become masters of their craft...pottery! Embark on an adventure, meet new clients, and master your pottery craft.

Clay Beats was created in a studio simulation with a thirty person team. This project was built in Unreal using Unreal blueprinting. I contributed to the team as a rhythm systems and multiplayer engineer.
