Gaming, Gaming & More Gaming

This project is a personal YouTube gaming channel focused on planning, recording, editing, and publishing both short-form and long-form gameplay content. The goal of the project is to build a consistent production pipeline while improving storytelling, pacing, and viewer engagement.

Through this project, I practice content strategy, basic video editing, thumbnail design, and performance analysis using YouTube Studio analytics. I regularly review metrics such as watch time, audience retention, and engagement to iterate on titles, thumbnails, and video structure.

This channel is treated as an ongoing learning project rather than a popularity-driven platform, emphasizing consistency, iteration, and measurable improvement over time.

I use CapCut and Adobe Premiere Pro for video editing, YouTube Studio for analytics, and OBS Studio and Steam's built-in recorder for recording.
