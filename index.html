About Me

My name is Reyna Murphy, and I am a recent graduate of the University of Utah. I majored in Computer Science with an Emphasis in Games. I currently work as an E-Commerce Associate at the University of Utah Campus Store.

More About Me

I became interested in computers after my brother built his own PC. Since then, I have discovered an interest in 3D modeling, programming, software development, and game development. To me, learning and understanding is the most important, and I am always striving to learn more and be introduced to more.

My hobbies include gaming, martial arts, soccer, hiking, LEGOs, and playing with my dogs.

Fun Facts:

  • My favorite type of LEGOs are the Speed Champion sets.
  • My favorite National Park is Glacier National Park.
