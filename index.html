About Me

My name is Reyna Murphy, and I am a recent graduate of the University of Utah. I majored in Computer Science with an Emphasis in Games. I currently work as an E-Commerce Associate at the University of Utah Campus Store.

More About Me

I became interested in computers after my brother built his own PC. Since then, I have discovered an interest in 3D modeling, programming, software development, web development, and game development.

My hobbies include gaming, martial arts, soccer, hiking, and LEGOs.
