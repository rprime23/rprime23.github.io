About Me

My name is Reyna Murphy, and I am a student at the University of Utah. I am majoring in Computer Science with an emphasis in games. I currently work as an ecommerce associate at the University of Utah Campus Store.

More About Me

To me, learning and understanding is the most important, and I am always striving to learn more and be introduced to more.

My hobbies include gaming, martial arts, soccer, hiking, and playing with my dogs.

My top skills are NetSuite, C++, C#, Java, Unreal Engine, and VS Code.
